# ĐỘBUCKS - Coffee & Pastry Website

A website introducing the coffee and pastry brand ĐỘBUCKS. This project is for **Exercise 3 - Lab 1**.

## Key Features

- **Home Page**: Automatic image slider, showcasing signature items and testimonials.
- **Menu Page**: Detailed catalog of over 16 drinks and pastries with high-quality illustrations.
- **Blog Page**: In-depth articles about coffee knowledge and brewing techniques.
- **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for Desktop, Tablet, and Mobile devices.
- **Performance**: Optimized images (WebP) and semantic HTML structure.
- **SEO Ready**: Meta tags and Open Graph implemented for all pages.

## Sitemap (List of Pages)

1. **Home (`index.html`)**: Brand introduction and featured signatures.
2. **About Us (`pages/about.html`)**: Brand story, vision, and mission.
3. **Menu (`pages/menu.html`)**: Detailed menu with prices and descriptions.
4. **Booking (`pages/booking.html`)**: Online table reservation system with real-time validation.
5. **Blog (`pages/blog.html`)**: Collection of articles and newsletters.
6. **Contact (`pages/contact.html`)**: Contact details for 3 branches, integrated My Maps, and feedback form.
7. **Article 1 (`pages/post1.html`)**: Insights into Signature Coffee varieties.
8. **Article 2 (`pages/post2.html`)**: The art of pairing coffee and pastries.
9. **Article 3 (`pages/post3.html`)**: Professional home brewing guide.
